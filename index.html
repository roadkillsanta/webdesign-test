About Me

Phzzl is a 24 year old Philly-based artist that uses fun rhythms and catchy melodies to make you want to dance, perfect for mass raves or concerts. He got into music after working a lot with Garageband for school, and started to play with sounds and beats in his late teens. Phzzl got his big break in 2014 with the release of his single, //NEW NAME PLS//, that got over 800,000 streams on SoundCloud. He really made himself known in 2016, when he topped the Billboard Hot 100 with his single Portrait. Phzzl has just dropped his album, Ladle, and is now preparing for his northeastern tour (tickets here).
